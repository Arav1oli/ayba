What is AYBA

An industry body built on trust, standards and shared expertise.

AYBA is the professional association for yacht brokers across Australasia. We exist to inspire, support and empower the people who make the yachting industry move — through recognised standards, trusted contracts, and a community of experienced professionals.

Membership means an active advocate for your business and the wider industry, a voice in the decisions that shape our profession, and access to the documents and networks that keep transactions safe and simple.

01

Members

A network of accredited brokers who uphold a shared code of conduct. Experienced members mentor those new to the trade, and every member has a say in how the association grows.

02

Committees

Working groups drawn from the membership steer standards, ethics and industry advocacy — keeping AYBA responsive to the needs of Australasian brokerage.

03

Documents

Standardised sale and purchase agreements, listing forms and best-practice guides. Sharing information broadens our field of vision and protects everyone at the table.

04

News & Events

Boat show dates, seminars, member events and industry updates — everything happening across the Australasian yachting calendar, in one place.
