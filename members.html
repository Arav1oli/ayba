A single place for member brokers to list, collaborate and transact. Sign-in opens once the platform is live.

What’s coming

Built to make listings effortless

The Members Area is where the association’s value comes to life. It’s in development now — here’s what members will get.

01

Your listings, auto-synced

Listings flow in from your existing feeds — no double-handling. Manage them from one backend.

02

Broker-only notes

Add private notes fellow members can see — price softness, what the owner will really entertain — kept off public display.

03

Co-brokerage deals

Surface your boats to the trusted network and split commission fairly when a member brings the buyer.

04

Broker-friendly downloads

Clean, brand-ready brochures and PDFs to push straight to your database.

05

Documents & contracts

The current standard agreements, always to hand.

06

Member directory

Know exactly who you’re dealing with — a vetted network you can trust.
